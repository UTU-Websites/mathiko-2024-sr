Hymns

English & Gikuyu

373 (342)

1 Safe in the arms of Jesus,
Safe on His gentle breast,
There by His love o’ershaded,
Sweetly my soul shall rest.
Hark! ’tis the voice of angels,
Borne in a song to me,
Over the fields of glory,
Over the jasper sea.
Chorus:
Safe in the arms of Jesus,
Safe on His gentle breast,
There by His love o’ershaded,
Sweetly my soul shall rest.
2 Safe in the arms of Jesus,
Safe from corroding care,
Safe from the world’s temptations,
Sin cannot harm me there.
Free from the blight of sorrow,
Free from my doubts and fears;
Only a few more trials,
Only a few more tears.
[Chorus]
3 Jesus, my heart’s dear refuge,
Jesus has died for me;
Firm on the Rock of Ages,
Ever my trust shall be.
Here let me wait with patience,
Wait till the night is o’er;
Wait till I see the morning
Break on the golden shore.
[Chorus]

304 (278)

1 Some day the silver cord will break,
And I no more as now shall sing;
But oh, the joy when I shall wake
Within the palace of the King!
2 Some day my earthly house will fall,
I cannot tell how soon ’twill be;
But this I know-—my All in All
Has now a place in heav’n for me.
[Chorus]
3 Some day, when fades the golden sun
Beneath the rosy-tinted west,
My blessed Lord will say, “Well done!”
And I shall enter into rest.
[Chorus]
4 Some day: till then I’ll watch and wait,
My lamp all trimmed and burning bright,
That when my Saviour opes the gate,
My soul to Him may take its flight.
[Chorus]

1 Jesũ We ũnyendete,
Nĩngũũrĩra harĩ we:
Rĩrĩa mbaara ĩĩ nene,
Nowe wa kũ’honokia;
’Hitha mbaru-inĩ ciaku,
Niĩ mũndũ mũthĩni;
’Honokia Ngai wakwa
Gitĩra muoyo wakwa.
2 Wĩhitho wakwa nowe
Na murũ wakwa nowe;
Ndikaingatwo harĩ we,
Njoya ũnjĩtĩkĩre;
Nowe ũteithio wakwa
O na mwĩgitio wakwa;
Ũndoorerie thũ ciakwa.
3 Wega wothe nĩ waku,
Wohanĩri no waku;
Mwathani ndanyihĩrũo,
Njiyũria wega waku:
Jesu ndakwamũkĩra,
We Githima kĩa muoyo;
Therũka thĩinĩ wakwa
Kinya tene na tene.
1 Niĩ nĩngwenda Ngai ũmenyage
Nĩngenaga mũno nĩwe;
Tondũ nĩũnjĩkaga wega
Na ũkanyenda hingo ciothe.
Chorus:
Irio ciakwa iria ndĩaga
Maĩ marĩa ngundaga;
O na nguo cia kwĩhumba
Ciothe nowe ũheaga.
2 Muoyo naguo nĩwe waheire
Nĩwe ũgiragia ngue;
Ũngethengia hinya waku
Ndingĩtinda gathaa kamwe.
3 We mũthenya o na ũtukũ
Nĩ ũmenyagĩrĩra wega;
Maitho maku no mambaraga
Kũrĩ ũtheri na kũrĩ nduna.
4 Ndingĩhota gũgũcokeria
Wega waku, Mwathani;
No nĩndakũhoya ũtũmage
Ngwende mũno na ngũiguage.
